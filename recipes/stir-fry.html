STIR-FRY NOODLES

DESCRIPTION

This dish is quick, colorful, and full of texture. The noodles soak up a savory soy-based sauce, while the chicken adds protein and flavor. Crunchy stir-fried vegetables like carrots, bell peppers, and cabbage bring freshness and a slight sweetness. Itâ€™s a perfect balance of chewy noodles, tender chicken, and crisp veggies all tossed in a glossy sauce.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 packs of noodles(Indomie, spaghetti, or Chinese egg noodles)
  • 1 chicken breast(sliced thin)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables(carrot, cabbage, green beans, bell peppers)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce(optional)
  • 2 cloves garlic(chopped)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp chili flakes(optional)

STEPS

  1. Boil noodles halfway (do not overcook), drain, and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a wok/pan, add garlic and stir-fry chicken until golden.
  3. Add vegetables, stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, and chili flakes.
  5. Toss in noodles, mix well for 2 minutes.
  6. Serve hot!
